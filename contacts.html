About Me

My Background

I began my professional career as a Registered Nurse, where I developed strong communication, teamwork, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills while caring for people in a variety of healthcare settings.

My passion for learning eventually led me into web development, where I discovered how much I enjoy creating clean, responsive, and user-friendly websites. I am currently building my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, databases, APIs, and responsive web design while continuing to expand my knowledge through practical projects.

My Interests

I enjoy solving problems, learning new technologies, reading books, cooking, exploring creative ideas, and continually improving both personally and professionally.

Outside of technology, I value spending quality time with family and friends and finding inspiration in everyday experiences. I believe curiosity and continuous learning are the foundation of both personal growth and successful software development.
